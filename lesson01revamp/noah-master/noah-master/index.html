About

Hey! My name is Michelle! I'm a full-time student studying Cybersecurity, and I love learning how technology works and how to keep systems safe and secure. I'm also really into web design and coding, and I enjoy creating websites that are clean, modern, and functional.

Outside of school, fitness is a huge part of my life. I love working out, training, and even competing in fitness competitions—it keeps me motivated, focused, and strong. I'm also a personal trainer on the side, helping people reach their goals and feel more confident in their bodies.

Family means everything to me. My sister and her three kids recently moved to San Diego, and I help take care of them—picking the kids up from school, spending time together, and supporting my sisters whenever I can.

I also love hanging out with my boyfriend, relaxing, and finding new places to eat because, honestly, I love food—especially trying new restaurants or anything with amazing flavor. When I get the chance, I love to travel to tropical places, soak up the sun, and just unwind.

I also work part-time doing billing and operations for my dad's pest control company. I'm passionate about growing, learning, and creating a life that blends my love for family, fitness, food, and technology.

What I Hope to Gain

Through this class, I hope to strengthen my technical and creative skills in web design and development. I want to learn how to build clean, functional, and visually appealing websites from the ground up.

Goals

I'm taking this class to develop my skills in website design and coding, which I've really come to enjoy. Learning how to create and design websites has opened a new creative outlet for me, and I'm excited to keep growing both personally and professionally.
