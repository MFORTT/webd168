About Me

Hello There!

My name is Michelle! I’m a full-time student studying Cybersecurity, and I love learning how technology works and how to keep systems safe and secure. I’m also really into web design and coding, and I enjoy creating websites that are clean, modern, and functional.

Outside of school, fitness is a huge part of my life. I love working out, training, and even competing in fitness competitions, it keeps me motivated, focused, and strong. I’m also a personal trainer on the side, helping people reach their goals and feel more confident in their bodies. I also work part-time doing billing and operations for my dad’s pest control company.

Family means everything to me. My sister and her three kids recently moved to San Diego, and I help take care of them, picking the kids up from school, spending time together, and supporting my sisters whenever I can. I also love hanging out with my boyfriend, relaxing, and finding new places to eat because, honestly, I love food, especially trying new restaurants or anything with amazing flavor. When I get the chance, I love to travel to tropical places, soak up the sun, and just unwind. I’m passionate about growing, learning, and creating a life that blends my love for family, fitness, food, and technology..
