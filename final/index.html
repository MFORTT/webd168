About Me

Transforming Ideas into Digital Reality

My journey in web development combines design thinking with technical skills the same discipline I bring to my fitness training. Just like perfecting a workout routine, I focus on building websites that are strong, efficient, and deliver results. Whether I'm coding responsive layouts or solving complex problems, I approach each project with the same dedication I bring to the gym: attention to detail, consistency, and a drive to improve.

As a fitness enthusiast and web developer based in San Diego, I work with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, SASS, Python, and Ruby on Rails to build functional web solutions. I understand that great user experiences like great workouts are about finding the right balance between form and function. When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me at the gym, trying new food, or working on projects like my Fitness Tracker application, where my two passions intersect!
